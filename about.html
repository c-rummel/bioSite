About

Sarah is a passionate person who loves her job, family, and traveling with both. Originally from Atlanta, she now lives in the central Florida area working at Walt Disney World. She has said many times that her favorite thing in the world is reading, but those who know her best would argue that her real favorite thing is working. She is very goal-oriented and loves to find a sense of accomplishment in anything she does.

When Sarah does take time for herself, she will often read period fantasy novels, and she loves to travel. Most often, she visits cities that have a rich history to them, domestically and abroad. She said, “my grandma always told me that we would go to Paris together. We were never able to, but I was finally able to go on my honeymoon. I loved the art, the architecture, and just being in a place where so many historical moments have happened.”

Cities are not the only places that call to Sarah. She also loves the outdoors. Spending time hiking, bicycling, and running round out the list of favorite activities. Most recently, she spent a week in the mountains of northern Georgia doing just those things. “I love being outside,” she said. “Especially when the weather is nice, it makes everything better. Walks are better, coffee tastes better, reading is better. I would love to move someplace more temperate so that spending time outside is easier to do year-round.”
